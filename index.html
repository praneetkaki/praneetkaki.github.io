INSIGHT

"Intervention Nurses Start Infants Growing on Healthy Trajectories (INSIGHT) is a study that analyzes the plausibility and potency of implementing a variety of early interventions that are intended to prevent childhood obesity. One facet of this study is sleep, as it was hypothesized that these interventions will lead to better sleep health/quality and increased physical activity. During my first semester in the lab, I helped with data entries for some of the device diaries that participants used to keep track of their general daily activity.

Website
EcoSleep

"EcoSleep is a study that analyzes the link between sleep and cognitive decline. Sleep is supposed to be an indicator of the onset of cognitive decline, especially among older people who are more prone to sleep and cognitive disorders. EMA (Ecological Momentary Assessments) are a series of cognitive tasks on a smart phone which are implemented in order to obtain daily cognitive data. During my first semester in the lab, I helped with data checks for the new EMA data for a couple of the smartphone cognitive tasks (EMA).

Website
Data4Action COVID-19 Study

"Data4Action is a county-wide (Centre County) study that is focused on documenting the impact that the Coronavirus pandemic had and continues to have on the lives of the residents. The PSU Sleep lab is involved in hair collection in order to perform cortisol analyses that can provide information about stress responses as a result of COVID-19. During my first semester, I helped make hair kits from home that I then shipped to the lab so that they could be used in the study.

Website
Einstein Aging Study

"The Einstein Aging Study (EAS) looks at the process of aging and how certain factors (such as sleep) are indicative of cognitive decline and dementias such as Alzheimer's Disease. The goal is to isolate these fators and develop interventions that can act upon them in order to slow down cognitive decline. Over this summer, I am going to be looking at actigraphy data (using the Spectrum software) and pulse oximetry data (using Nonin software) for data checks as they are uploaded by the team at Albert Einstein College of Medicine.
